# task_09_DA
Sales Trend Analysis using SQL Aggregation techniques.

Business Performance Summary (Jan–Aug 2024):
Total revenue peaked in January (₹14,548.32) and showed a downward trend, reaching the lowest in July (₹6,797.08). A slight recovery occurred in August.
Electronics is the best-performing category overall, generating ₹34,982.41, followed by Home Appliances (₹18,646.16) and Sports (₹14,326.51).
Top-selling product each month is consistently a premium electronics item, showing strong consumer demand for high-value tech products.
Order volumes were consistent (around 29–31 orders monthly), but average units per order and average order value declined, indicating lower customer spending over time.

Regional Insights:

North America is the highest revenue-generating region (₹36,844.34), followed by Asia (₹22,455.45) and Europe (₹21,268.06).
Region-wise strengths:
North America: Dominated by Electronics
Europe: Strong in Home Appliances
Asia: Strong in Sports & Clothing

Payment Behaviour:
Credit Card is the most preferred payment method throughout all months.
PayPal and Debit Card volumes remained stable with lower usage.

Product Category Trends:
Electronics was the top-selling category for 6 out of 8 months.
Clothing dominated in units sold (quantity), especially Feb–Jul, indicating high volume but low price-point sales.
Books had the highest quantity in August, showing seasonal shift.

Overall Summary:
The business performed strongly in early 2024, with Electronics as the major revenue engine. However, sales and average order value gradually declined, indicating possible seasonal impact or customer price sensitivity. Segmented marketing, pricing strategy adjustments, and targeted product focus can help improve performance in upcoming months.
